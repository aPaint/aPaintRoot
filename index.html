Latest Version Notes

Version 1.0.0

Dear users,

In these unusual times of Covid-19, online teaching has become more prevalent and part of our regular learning experience. This has resulted in less physical and face-to-face interactions. To spice up our times in front of our laptops at home, I developed this program that would allow us to draw on the screen from the air. The programming experience was extremely exciting for me, if you would like to learn how I made it, I would love to share the resources I used to learn about image processing!

This is the very first version of aPaint. While I am very proud and excited to finally publish this piece of software, it is still a very simple program that is lacking many features. Please feel free to send feedback, whether it be new features or bug fixes!
